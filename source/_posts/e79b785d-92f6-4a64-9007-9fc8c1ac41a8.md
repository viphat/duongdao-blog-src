**Like a daisy
simple but comfy**


**when a petal**


**drops,**


**it still live gorgeously**


**So do you,**


**losing a part of yourself**


**not making you broken down,**


**it will be dashingly**


**fade out**


**and You**


**in totally**


**always something to-be-proud**
